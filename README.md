# Ex-4.-Scenario-Based-Report-Development-Utilizing-Diverse-Prompting-Techniques
Objective: The goal of this experiment is to design and develop an AI-powered chatbot that can handle customer inquiries, provide support, and improve customer experience in a retail environment. Create prompts using various AI prompting techniques to guide your experiment, data collection, analysis, and report creation.
## Aim: 
To design an AI-based retail chatbot called RetailBot 2.0 that helps customers, improves sales, provides quick support, and makes retail operations more efficient.

## Algorithm: 
### Refined Prompt: The Retail AI Architect Framework

**Role:** Act as an expert AI Prompt Engineer and Retail Systems Architect.

**Objective:** Design a comprehensive blueprint and functional logic for a retail-specific chatbot ("RetailBot 2.0") aimed at maximizing customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

**Task:** Please execute the following sequence of development tasks:

**1. Persona & Voice Architecture:**

* Define a retail-specific persona that balances professional efficiency with a friendly, helpful brand voice.
* Establish a "Style Guide" (e.g., maximum sentence length, use of emojis, and tone shift protocols when handling complaints).

**2. Multi-Technique Scenario Design:**

* **Chain-of-Thought (CoT):** Detail the internal reasoning steps the bot must take to resolve a "Missing Package" inquiry, including verification, logistics API checks, and resolution options.
* **Few-Shot Prompting:** Provide three examples of "High-Value Upselling"—showing how the bot should transition from a basic product query to a personalized recommendation.
* **Contextual Grounding:** Create a protocol for how the bot handles "Out of Stock" items without losing the sale (e.g., suggesting alternatives or "Notify Me" options).

**3. Data Experimentation & Analysis Plan:**

* Outline a strategy for A/B testing two different response styles (e.g., "Direct & Concise" vs. "Empathetic & Detailed").
* List the specific metrics (e.g., Deflection Rate, Sentiment Score, Conversion Rate) required to evaluate the chatbot's performance.

**4. Guardrails & Safety Logic:**

* Define strict constraints to prevent the AI from hallucinating prices or making unauthorized promises.
* Establish a "Human-in-the-Loop" trigger system—exactly when and how the AI should hand off a conversation to a live agent.

**Formatting Instructions:**

* Use **Markdown headers** for each section.
* Present the logic workflows in **bulleted lists**.
* Ensure the final output serves as a "Master Prompt" that could be used to program the actual chatbot interface.
